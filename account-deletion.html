Account & Data Deletion Request

If you have created an account on the TheIronKingdom app and wish to delete your account and all associated data, you can request deletion at any time.

How to Request Account Deletion

Please send an email to the address below with the subject: “Account Deletion Request – TheIronKingdom”

Email: codemobile.prod.app2025@gmail.com

Your email must include:

  • Your phone number used for login
  • A request to delete all associated data
  • Optional: Reason for deletion

Processing Time

We will process your request within 7 business days and notify you once the deletion is complete.

What Data Will Be Deleted?

Upon request, we will delete all personal data associated with your account, including:

  • Phone number
  • User profile data
  • Any data stored on our backend servers

Contact

If you have any questions regarding data deletion, email us at:

codemobile.prod.app2025@gmail.com
